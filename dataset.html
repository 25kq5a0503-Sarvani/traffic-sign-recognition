About GTSRB

One of the most widely used benchmarks for traffic sign classification

📌 Dataset Details

  • Name: German Traffic Sign Recognition Benchmark
  • Total classes: 43 types of traffic signs
  • Training images: ~39,000+
  • Test images: ~12,000+
  • Image format: PPM / PNG
  • Source: Kaggle / GTSRB official

⚙️ Preprocessing Steps

  • Resize all images to 32×32 pixels
  • Convert to RGB format
  • Normalize pixel values: 0–255 → 0–1
  • One-hot encode class labels
  • Split: 80% train / 20% test
  • Random state = 42 (reproducibility)

Sign Categories

43 classes of German traffic signs

🔴 Prohibitory Signs

Speed limits, No passing, No entry, No vehicles — signs that restrict or forbid certain actions.

⚠️ Warning Signs

Bumpy road, Slippery road, Children crossing, Wild animals — signs alerting drivers to hazards ahead.

🔵 Mandatory Signs

Keep right, Roundabout, Ahead only — signs indicating what drivers must do.

🟡 Priority Signs

Yield, Priority road, Right-of-way — signs that control traffic flow at intersections.

🟢 Information Signs

Road work, Traffic signals, General caution — signs that provide important information to drivers.

⬜ End Signs

End of speed limits, End of no passing — signs cancelling previous restrictions.

📁 How to Add Dataset

1. Download GTSRB from Kaggle
2. Extract the ZIP file
3. Place the Train/ folder inside dataset/
4. Structure should be: dataset/Train/0/, dataset/Train/1/ ... dataset/Train/42/
5. Go to Train page and click Start Training
