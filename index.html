Why Traffic Sign Recognition?

Manual identification of signs fails at high speeds. AI makes it instant and accurate.

🚗 Road Safety

Automated sign recognition helps drivers and autonomous vehicles react faster to road conditions, reducing accidents.

🤖 Self-Driving Cars

Every autonomous vehicle depends on real-time traffic sign classification to navigate safely on public roads.

📷 Computer Vision

This project demonstrates how CNN models can understand visual information just like the human eye.

How It Works

4 simple steps from image to prediction

📂

1. Load Dataset

50,000+ GTSRB images across 43 sign categories are loaded and preprocessed.

🧠

2. Train CNN

A Convolutional Neural Network learns patterns from thousands of sign images.

📤

3. Upload Image

User uploads any traffic sign image through the web interface.

✅

4. Get Result

Model instantly predicts the sign name with confidence percentage.
